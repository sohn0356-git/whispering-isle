# Game Design Overview

## Core Loop
1. **Exploration** – Wander through handcrafted biomes to discover stories, resources, and changes in the island mood.
2. **NPC Interactions** – Check in with villagers, listen to their needs, deliver comfort items, and build trust.
3. **Quests** – Accept restorative tasks that nudge the main narrative and unlock new systems.
4. **Collectibles** – Recover heirlooms, nature samples, and memory tokens that flesh out the lore.
5. **Abilities** – Earn traversal tools, crafting tricks, or social perks that reopen earlier spaces and restart the loop.

## Atmosphere & Tone
The isle should feel gentle, curious, and lived-in: soft lighting, organic UI, and soundscapes of distant waves reinforce calm exploration. Stakes come from emotional investment rather than danger, so even urgent beats lean on empathy, humor, and quiet wonder.

## High-Level Player Goals
- Reconnect each region’s guardian spirit by completing their restorative questlines.
- Help villagers feel at home by finishing personal story arcs and unlocking community upgrades.
- Catalog the island’s flora, fauna, and artifacts for the Whispering Almanac.
- Restore shared spaces (docks, markets, observatories) that open trade and travel options.
- Personalize the player’s cottage and workshop as a reflection of progress.
