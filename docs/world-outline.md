# World Outline

## Mistveil Harbor
- **Theme:** Fog-kissed docks and floating markets where new arrivals settle.
- **Landmarks:** Lighthouse of Echoes, Tidal Archive barge, kelp-powered ferry.
- **Traversal Needs:** Basic sailing lessons and the Tide Compass unlock access to offshore platforms.

## Sunpetal Terraces
- **Theme:** Cascading fields and greenhouses basked in perpetual sunset hues.
- **Landmarks:** Pollinator Conservatory, Terraced Tea House, Skyseed Windmills.
- **Traversal Needs:** Glide Cloak allows safe descent between terraces and wind currents.

## Luminous Caverns
- **Theme:** Bioluminescent caves with crystalline pools and subterranean reefs.
- **Landmarks:** Prism Choir Chamber, Glowcap Nursery, Subtidal Observatory.
- **Traversal Needs:** Lumenshroom Lantern and diving bell for underwater passages.

## Whispering Canopy
- **Theme:** Elevated rainforest canopy stitched by treehouse bridges and songstones.
- **Landmarks:** Canopy Council platform, Choral Vines, Aeolian Archives.
- **Traversal Needs:** Vine Grapple lets players swing between tree clusters and reach secret nests.

## Embercliff Ridge
- **Theme:** Warm volcanic cliffs repurposed into hot spring retreats and kiln workshops.
- **Landmarks:** Ember Baths, Clay Harmonium kilns, Firefly Observatory.
- **Traversal Needs:** Heatshield charm and climbing axes to traverse fumarole vents safely.
