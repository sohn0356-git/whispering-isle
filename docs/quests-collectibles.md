# Quests & Collectibles

## Quest Concepts
1. **Harbor Harmonies:** Restore tide chimes scattered along the docks to calm the nightly foghorn chorus.
2. **Garden of Borrowed Light:** Help Jonas cultivate glowvines that illuminate the Sunpetal Terraces during eclipses.
3. **Letters Across the Canopy:** Assist Lale in delivering heartfelt letters between canopy dwellers to reconnect estranged friends.

## Collectible Categories
- **Memory Shells:** Audio snippets of island history that unlock ambient tracks.
- **Lumenshards:** Crystals used to craft lantern upgrades and guide players in dark regions.
- **Heritage Trinkets:** Personal heirlooms from villagers that decorate community spaces and boost relationship tiers.
